Succulent Pork Tenderloin wrapped in Prosciutto

This recipe features a tender pork tenderloin wrapped in savory prosciutto, creating a delicious and elegant dish perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork tenderloin
  • 6 ounces of prosciutto
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 bunch fresh sage, leaves removed and stems discarded, divided
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  4. Add sage leaves to the skillet and transfer it to the preheated oven.

  6. Let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
