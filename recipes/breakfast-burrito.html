Delicious Breakfast Burrito

This recipe features a hearty breakfast burrito filled with scrambled eggs, cheese, and your choice of meats and vegetables, wrapped in a warm tortilla.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 1/2 cup cooked sausage or bacon, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers and onions
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • Salsa or hot sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add the diced bell peppers and onions. Saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the egg mixture to the skillet and cook, stirring gently until the eggs are scrambled and cooked through.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in the cheese and crumbled sausage or bacon.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  6. Spoon the egg mixture onto each tortilla, roll them up tightly, and serve with salsa or hot sauce if desired.

Enjoy your delicious breakfast burrito!
